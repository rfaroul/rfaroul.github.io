About Me

Hello! My name is Rachelle and I divide my time between Philadelphia, PA and Brooklyn, NY, where I'm from. I have a strong background in program and project management, food buying, and economic development. When I left the food and agriculture industry last Summer to learn to code, I had no idea where I'd end up. All I knew was that what I enjoy most about technology is that it's a powerful tool in the fight for social and economic justice. I'm eager to explore the ways in which the presentation of data shapes opinions, policies, and decision-making in today's increasingly global society.

Check out what I've learned and made in a few short months, and feel free to get in touch via LinkedIn, phone or email. I'm looking for opportunities to show off my coding skills! When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me farming, riding my bike, practicing the turntables, or hosting dinner parties.
